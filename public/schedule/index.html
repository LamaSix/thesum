Kanyi Maqubela

The True Cult of Innovation

Kanyi will unpack two popular phrases in the creative community: "Failure is good" and "Do what you love". These are chanted as mantra, with some strongly unintended consequences. As a product of failure, and lover of innovation, he speaks from personal experience as an entrepreneur and investor about the true nature of innovation.

11:25am

Clark Scheffy

Culture of Play

In this talk, Clark Scheffy, a managing director at IDEO's San Francisco location, will present his point of view around the the importance of play and creativity in the workplace. Clark will share recent examples of how to incorporate humor in day-to-day work. When solving complex challenges, designers at IDEO have found that focusing on the problem alone can be unproductive. Injecting elements of play and humor can be exactly what reveals the creative solution needed.
